Our Story

From San Jose to the Puget Sound

Cordon Painting was founded in 2018 by Giovani Cordon, shortly after relocating from San Jose, California to the Pacific Northwest. With little more than a set of tools, a strong work ethic, and a commitment to doing things right, Giovani built the business from the ground up — one job at a time.

For the first two years, it was just Giovani. Every brushstroke, every estimate, every client relationship — handled personally. That dedication to quality and care became the foundation that Cordon Painting is still built on today.

In 2020, Giovani's grandson Sebastian joined the team, bringing new energy and a shared passion for the craft. Together, they've grown Cordon Painting into a trusted name across Tacoma, Seattle, and the Greater Puget Sound.

Who We Are

A Family Business, Through and Through

We're not a large corporation. We're a family — and that means every client gets treated like a neighbor. When you hire Cordon Painting, you're working directly with people who take pride in their name on every job.

We serve homeowners, property managers, and businesses across the Greater Puget Sound. Whether it's a single room or a full apartment complex, we show up on time, do the work with care, and don't leave until you're satisfied.
